ABOUT THE SCHOOL

St. Joseph’s Public School

St. Joseph's School, established in June 2000 in the Malakpet area of Hyderabad, is a part of the prestigious St. Joseph's Group of Schools, founded by late Sri U. G. Reddy in 1971. Over the years, the group has grown to encompass numerous educational institutions across the twin cities, building a legacy of excellence in education.

The school offers a structured 12-year educational journey with two years of Preparatory Education (Preparatory 1 and Preparatory 2) nurturing early learning and social development, followed by Primary School (Classes I to V) and Senior School (Classes VI to X) preparing students for the ICSE (Indian Certificate of Secondary Education) Examination, affiliated with CISCE, New Delhi.

English is the medium of instruction, and the curriculum is designed to encourage curiosity, creativity, and independent thinking, preparing children for their journey into formal schooling under the guidance of able and experienced teachers.

The school's mission goes beyond academic instruction, emphasizing the intellectual, moral, emotional, physical, and aesthetic growth of each student.

Though academic excellence is the main aim of our institution, it is necessary to expose the students to the outside world to relax and think. To this end, the activities of various Clubs like the Science Club, Eco Club, Quiz Club, and Literary Club foster creativity, critical thinking, and leadership. Students regularly participate in prestigious ASISC Literary and Cultural Competitions at Zonal, Regional, and National Levels giving them an opportunity to showcase their talents on larger platforms and gain national exposure. Our students are also encouraged to participate in various Olympiads conducted by ISFO, Unified Council, and Maths Olympiad.

The school also emphasises the importance of Physical Education and Sports, and encourages students to actively participate in a wide range of sports, including Basketball, Cricket, Volleyball, Karate, Kho-Kho and many more. Additionally, indoor games such as Chess and Carrom are equally promoted. These games are not only practised within the school but are also played at inter-school levels, allowing students to hone their skills in a competitive environment.

Our students have the opportunity to participate and excel in the Regional and National Level CISCE Games and Sports events, with many advancing to the esteemed SGFI National Games and Sports.

Introduced in 2021-2022, the NCC Army Wing (JD & JW) under the 5 Telangana Battalion is a hallmark of the school’s commitment to discipline and leadership. This program instills a sense of national pride and equips cadets with the skills and resilience needed to face future challenges, shaping them into responsible and committed citizens of the country.

Field trips, picnics, annual excursions to places of historical and cultural significance enrich students' understanding of India's heritage, further enriching their learning experience beyond the classroom.

Democratic principles are instilled through an active School Cabinet and Parliament comprising the President, the Prime Minister, and the Council of Ministers. This platform grooms future leaders by addressing school issues and implementing innovative ideas. The functioning of the School Cabinet and School Parliament in our institution has been highly successful in laying a strong foundation for future parliamentarians and legislators.

Through its blend of academics, extracurriculars, and leadership opportunities, St. Joseph's School nurtures well-rounded individuals poised to excel in a rapidly changing world. The school continues its mission of empowering young minds, upholding its legacy of distinction within the St. Joseph's Group of Schools.h's Group of Schools.
