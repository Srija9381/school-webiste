Science Labs

BIOLOGY LAB

At Gloria Vidhya Kendra School, our Biology lab serves as a dynamic hub for scientific exploration, inviting students into the captivating realm of life sciences. Fitted with state-of-the-art equipment, the lab offers an immersive experience where students delve into hands-on experiments and investigations. Under the guidance of skilled educators, students explore biological concepts, conduct dissections, and engage in practical learning that reinforces theoretical knowledge. Through interactive sessions and collaborative projects, students develop critical thinking skills, a scientific mindset, and a profound appreciation for the intricate web of life. The Biology lab at our school is a vital space where curiosity blossoms into a lifelong fascination with the wonders of the living world.

CHEMISTRY LAB

Gloria Vidhya Kendra School takes pride in its advanced Physics lab, providing students with a dynamic space for hands-on experimentation and experiential learning. Our state-of-the-art lab is equipped with modern apparatus, fostering a practical understanding of theoretical concepts. Students engage in a spectrum of experiments, exploring the principles of physics in a collaborative and innovative environment. Beyond traditional textbook learning, the lab experience enhances critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a profound appreciation for the laws governing the physical world. Under the guidance of experienced educators, students unravel the mysteries of physics, laying the foundation for future scientific endeavors. The Physics lab at Gloria Vidhya Kendra School is a vibrant hub where curiosity meets discovery, preparing students for academic excellence and a lifelong fascination with the wonders of the physical universe.

PHYSICS LAB

Gloria Vidhya Kendra School boasts a cutting-edge Chemistry lab, a hub of scientific exploration that transcends theoretical boundaries. In this innovative space, students embark on a journey of hands-on experimentation, unveiling the captivating world of chemical reactions and molecular structures. Our well-equipped lab facilitates a dynamic learning experience, enabling students to apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios. Under the guidance of experienced educators, students conduct diverse experiments, honing analytical skills and developing a profound understanding of chemical principles. The Chemistry lab at our school goes beyond traditional learning, fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and a deep appreciation for the wonders of chemistry. It stands as a testament to our commitment to providing a holistic and enriching education, preparing students for future scientific pursuits and real-world applications
