Corrospondent’s Message

As students embark on their educational journey at Gloria Vidya Kendram E M School, the hope is that they will not only gain knowledge but also grow into responsible and compassionate individuals. The overarching goal is to lay the foundation for a well-rounded education that prepares students for the challenges of the world while instilling values that will guide them throughout their lives. In this endeavor, the school extends its blessings to all, seeking to create an environment where every child can flourish and reach their full potential.

- Fr Thomas Pulickal
